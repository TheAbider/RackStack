# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the project and its users

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information
- Other conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All complaints will be reviewed and will result in a response deemed appropriate to the circumstances.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other communication channels associated with this project.
